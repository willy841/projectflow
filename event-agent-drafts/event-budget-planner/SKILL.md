# Event Budget Planner

Give the plan cost structure and tradeoff logic.

## Workflow

1. Confirm whether the budget is fixed, target-based, or still open.
2. Break costs into venue, F&B, stage/AV, visuals, talent, gifts, staffing, logistics, contingency, and admin.
3. Identify major cost drivers.
4. Mark which items are must-protect, flexible, or optional.
5. Offer at least one leaner and one richer scope scenario when useful.
6. Make assumptions explicit.

## Output Format

1. Budget posture
2. Cost categories
3. Major cost drivers
4. Protect vs flexible items
5. Scenario options
6. Assumptions
7. Financial risks

## Boundaries

- Do not invent vendor quotes.
- Do not present estimates as confirmed prices.
- Do not ignore contingency reserve.
